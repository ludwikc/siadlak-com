SEO Audit Report

siadlak.com — Full Site Audit
March 28, 2026

Executive Summary

siadlak.com has a solid SEO foundation with thoughtful meta tag configuration, structured data via JSON-LD, a prerendering pipeline for SPA crawlability, and well-structured URL paths. However, the site has several critical gaps preventing it from capturing organic traffic: no XML sitemap referenced in robots.txt, missing Polish diacritics in all meta descriptions (hurting relevance matching), no blog or indexable content layer for top-of-funnel keywords, and the site is a client-side SPA with prerendering that may not fully solve JavaScript rendering issues for all crawlers.

Biggest strength: Clean URL architecture, comprehensive per-page SEO config with unique titles/descriptions, and structured data on all key pages.

Top 3 priorities:

1. Fix meta descriptions — all descriptions use ASCII transliterations (e.g., "produktywnosci" instead of "produktywności"), which hurts keyword matching for Polish search queries.
2. Add XML sitemap and reference it in robots.txt — without it, Google has no authoritative URL list for this SPA.
3. Build a content layer (blog/articles) — the site has zero indexable informational content, leaving all top-of-funnel keyword traffic on the table.

Content Gap Analysis

1. No Blog / Article Layer

This is the most significant content gap. The site has excellent conversion pages (program descriptions, discovery, about) but zero indexable informational content. Competitors like wiktortokarski.pl have 50+ blog posts ranking for "coaching", "rozwój osobisty", "jak zwiększyć produktywność" and similar queries. Without blog content, siadlak.com cannot capture any top-of-funnel organic traffic.

Priority: Critical   Effort: Substantial (ongoing)   Format: Blog section at /blog or /artykuly

2. Missing FAQ Schema on FAQ-Heavy Pages

The Life OS page has 7 excellent FAQs. The Discovery page answers 3 key questions. None use FAQPage schema — missing rich FAQ snippets in SERPs that could dramatically increase click-through rate.

Priority: Quick Win   Effort: 1-2 hours   Format: Add JSON-LD FAQPage schema to existing pages

3. No Comparison Content

Competitors publish "coaching vs terapia", "coaching vs mentoring", "jaki coach wybrać" comparison articles. These rank well for decision-stage queries. siadlak.com's Life OS FAQ addresses "Jak to się różni od terapii?" but it's buried in a page, not a standalone indexable article.

Priority: High   Effort: Half day per article   Format: Blog comparison posts

4. Podcast Episodes Not Individually Indexed

Life Hacking Podcast and Uważne Życie podcast have episode lists but individual episodes don't have their own URLs/pages. Each episode is a potential long-tail keyword target. Competitors who transcribe and index podcast episodes capture significant organic traffic.

Priority: Strategic   Effort: Substantial   Format: Individual episode pages with transcripts at /podcast/life-hacking/[episode-slug]

5. No "Free Resources" Landing Pages

The site mentions DeepWork.pl and UwazneZycie.pl but doesn't create optimized landing pages about these tools on siadlak.com itself. "Deep work" and "praca w skupieniu" are searchable terms that could bring qualified traffic.

Priority: Medium   Effort: Moderate   Format: Dedicated resource pages on siadlak.com

6. Missing "About Coaching" Educational Content

Competitors dominate educational queries: "co to jest coaching", "jak działa coaching", "ile kosztuje coaching". siadlak.com doesn't have any educational content explaining coaching concepts — it goes straight to selling programs. Adding educational content builds topical authority.

Priority: Strategic   Effort: Substantial   Format: Pillar page + topic cluster

Prioritized Action Plan

Quick Wins (This Week)

1. Fix all Polish diacritics in seo-config.ts

Replace every ASCII-transliterated Polish word with proper diacritics across all meta descriptions and keywords. Single file change, massive impact on keyword matching.

Quick Win Impact: High · Effort: 1 hour · File: src/lib/seo-config.ts
2. Fix static index.html fallback to Polish

Change the <title> and <meta name="description"> in index.html from English to Polish to match the Helmet-injected values.

Quick Win Impact: High · Effort: 15 minutes · File: index.html
3. Generate and add XML sitemap

Create a sitemap.xml in public/ listing all indexable routes with <lastmod> dates. Add Sitemap: https://siadlak.com/sitemap.xml to robots.txt. Consider auto-generating from the seoRouteConfig array.

Quick Win Impact: High · Effort: 1-2 hours
4. Add noindex to test/archive pages

Mark /index2025, /index2601, /home-test, /home-test2, /home-test3 as noindex in SEO config. Better yet: remove them from production routes entirely.

Quick Win Impact: Medium · Effort: 30 minutes
5. Add FAQPage schema to Life OS and Discovery pages

Wrap existing FAQ content in FAQPage JSON-LD schema. Life OS has 7 Q&As, Discovery has 3. These could show as rich results in SERPs.

Quick Win Impact: Medium · Effort: 1 hour
6. Shorten title tag suffix

Change defaultSEO.title in SEO.tsx to something shorter (e.g., "Ludwik C. Siadlak") so combined titles stay under 60 characters.

Quick Win Impact: Medium · Effort: 15 minutes
7. Fix prerender route mismatches

Update scripts/prerender.mjs to use correct route paths: /program/produktywnosc (not hakowanie-produktywnosci), /program/odpornosc (not silna-glowa), /program/meskosc (not meski-kompas), /program/uwaznosc (not uwazne-zycie).

Quick Win Impact: Medium · Effort: 15 minutes

Strategic Investments (This Quarter)

8. Launch a blog / article section

Create /blog route with 2-3 pillar articles targeting high-opportunity keywords: "jak zwiększyć produktywność" (pillar), "mindfulness dla programistów", "odporność psychiczna ćwiczenia". Publish 2-4 posts per month. Use Ludwik's newsletter content as a starting point — repurpose for SEO.

Strategic Impact: Very High · Effort: Ongoing · Dependency: Content workflow
9. Add Course schema to all program pages

Implement Course structured data with price, duration, provider, and aggregate rating. This enables rich course snippets in search results — a significant competitive advantage.

Strategic Impact: High · Effort: Half day
10. Create individual podcast episode pages with transcripts

Each episode gets its own URL with show notes and transcript. Transcripts are indexable text that targets long-tail keywords. Use AI transcription to scale this efficiently.

Strategic Impact: High · Effort: Substantial · Dependency: Transcript generation
11. Build a "coaching vs X" comparison content cluster

Publish 3-5 comparison articles: coaching vs terapia, coaching vs mentoring, coaching online vs stacjonarny, coaching indywidualny vs grupowy. These target decision-stage queries with strong commercial intent.

Strategic Impact: High · Effort: 1 day per article
12. Evaluate SSR migration or hybrid approach

While prerendering works, server-side rendering (Next.js, Astro, or Vite SSR) would ensure every page is always crawlable. Evaluate effort vs. reward — if organic traffic becomes a priority, SSR is the most reliable foundation.

Strategic Impact: Medium · Effort: Substantial · Dependency: Architecture decision
13. Defer non-critical third-party scripts

Move MailerLite, CookieYes, and GA scripts to load after page content. Use defer or dynamic injection after DOMContentLoaded. This improves LCP and FID Core Web Vitals.

Strategic Impact: Medium · Effort: 2-3 hours
